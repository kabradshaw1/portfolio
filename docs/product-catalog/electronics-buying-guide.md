## Monitors: 27" 4K Monitor

The **27" 4K Monitor** delivers professional-grade display quality suitable for design work, coding, and media consumption.

### Display Quality

- **Resolution:** 3840x2160 (4K UHD) on a 27" IPS panel — approximately 163 PPI for sharp text and detail
- **Color accuracy:** 99% sRGB coverage is sufficient for web and UI design; not a wide-gamut display for print work
- **Brightness:** 350 nits is comfortable for office environments; manage reflections with monitor placement rather than brightness

### Connectivity

- **USB-C PD 65W:** One cable handles video input + charges a connected laptop. Ideal for laptop users who want a clean desk
- **HDMI 2.0:** Supports 4K at 60Hz for desktop and gaming use
- **DisplayPort 1.4:** Higher bandwidth for future-proofing with high refresh rate or HDR content

### Ergonomics

Height adjustment, tilt, swivel, and pivot (portrait mode) mean the monitor adapts to your workspace rather than forcing you to adapt to it. Portrait mode is valuable for reading documents or long code files.

### Pairing Recommendations

Use with the **Laptop Pro 15"** over USB-C for a single-cable docking setup. Add the **USB-C Hub 7-in-1** downstream for additional USB-A ports.

## Audio: Wireless Bluetooth Headphones & Wireless Earbuds Pro

### Wireless Bluetooth Headphones

Over-ear noise-canceling design for extended listening sessions at a desk or while traveling.

- **Battery:** 30-hour rated life; easily covers a full workweek of 8-hour days between charges
- **Noise cancellation:** Active noise canceling (ANC) excels at blocking consistent background noise like HVAC, traffic, and open-office chatter
- **Comfort:** Over-ear cushions distribute clamping pressure across a larger surface area — preferred for all-day wear versus on-ear designs
- **Best for:** Focus work, video calls, travel

### Wireless Earbuds Pro

Compact in-ear alternative with active noise canceling.

- **Battery:** 8-hour bud battery + wireless charging case for extended use away from outlets
- **ANC:** In-ear seal combined with ANC is particularly effective at isolating high-frequency noise
- **Wireless charging case:** Drop the case on any Qi pad at the end of the day
- **Best for:** Commuting, gym, situations where over-ear headphones are impractical

### Choosing Between Them

| Factor | Wireless Bluetooth Headphones | Wireless Earbuds Pro |
|---|---|---|
| All-day comfort | Better (over-ear cushion) | Good (depends on fit) |
| Portability | Moderate (foldable) | Excellent (pocket-sized case) |
| Battery (buds only) | 30 hours | 8 hours |
| Workout use | Not ideal | Yes |

## Networking: WiFi 6 Router

The **WiFi 6 Router** upgrades your home or office network to the latest AX3000 standard.

### Why Upgrade to WiFi 6

- **MU-MIMO:** Serves multiple devices simultaneously rather than sequentially — critical in households with 10+ connected devices
- **OFDMA:** Subdivides channels to reduce latency for small data transfers (smart home devices, gaming)
- **WPA3:** Stronger encryption than WPA2, including protection against offline dictionary attacks
- **Real-world throughput:** Dual-band AX3000 (600 Mbps at 2.4 GHz + 2400 Mbps at 5 GHz) vs. typical AC1200 routers

### Setup Considerations

- Place centrally and elevated — WiFi signals spread outward and downward from the antenna
- 4x Gigabit LAN ports support wired connections for desktops, smart TVs, and gaming consoles where stability matters more than convenience

## Accessories

### Portable SSD 1TB

The **Portable SSD 1TB** provides fast, reliable external storage for backups, media libraries, and transferring large files.

- **Speed:** Up to 1050 MB/s read via USB-C — transfers a 10GB file in roughly 10 seconds
- **Form factor:** Pocket-sized and bus-powered; no external power required
- **Durability:** NVMe flash storage has no moving parts; more shock-resistant than traditional hard drives
- **Use cases:** Time Machine backup for Mac users, video footage storage, portable development environment

### Mechanical Keyboard

The **Mechanical Keyboard** delivers tactile and audible feedback that many developers and writers prefer over membrane keyboards.

- **Switches:** Cherry MX switches are the industry standard — well-documented actuation force and travel distance
- **RGB backlighting:** Per-key RGB lighting with programmable profiles
- **Build quality:** Mechanical keyboards typically last 50 million keystrokes per switch versus 5-10 million for membrane
- **Noise consideration:** If you work near others, Cherry MX Brown (tactile, quieter) or Silent Red variants are available options to discuss with our team

### USB-C Hub 7-in-1

The **USB-C Hub 7-in-1** is the connectivity solution for thin laptops with limited ports.

- **HDMI output:** 4K resolution for connecting to monitors and projectors
- **USB-A x3:** Connect mice, keyboards, USB drives, and other legacy peripherals
- **SD and microSD card slots:** Direct card access for photographers and videographers
- **PD 100W passthrough:** Charges your laptop at full speed while all other ports are in use
