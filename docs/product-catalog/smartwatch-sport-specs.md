### Dual-Band GPS Detail

Dual-band GPS (L1 + L5 frequencies) significantly improves position accuracy in challenging environments:
- **Urban canyons:** Multi-path signal reflection from buildings is reduced
- **Tree cover:** L5 band penetrates canopy more effectively
- **Typical accuracy:** 2-5 meters vs. 10-15 meters for single-band GPS

## Water Resistance

| Specification | Value |
|---|---|
| Rating | 5ATM (50 meters) |
| Swimming | Safe for pool and open-water swimming |
| Showering | Yes |
| Diving | Not rated for scuba or high-velocity water sports |

5ATM (50-meter water resistance) means the watch is tested at 50 meters of static water pressure. This is suitable for recreational swimming, lap swimming, snorkeling, and showering. It is not intended for scuba diving or high-speed water sports where dynamic pressure exceeds the static rating.

## Battery Life

| Mode | Battery Life |
|---|---|
| Smartwatch mode (AOD off) | Up to 7 days |
| Smartwatch mode (AOD on) | Up to 5 days |
| GPS mode | Up to 18 hours |
| GPS + music playback | Up to 12 hours |
| Sleep tracking only | Up to 14 days |

### Battery Notes

Estimated battery life is based on typical usage patterns. The following factors reduce battery life:
- Always-on display (approximately 30% reduction)
- Continuous GPS recording
- High-frequency heart rate monitoring
- Bright environments triggering high brightness levels
- Active Bluetooth streaming
